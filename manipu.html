Popular Destinations

Imphal

Imphal is the capital city of the Indian state of Manipur. It is the second largest city in northeast India after Guwahati and Agartala.

Loktak lake

Loktak Lake is, without a doubt, one of the most iconic features in northeast India.

Keibul Lamjao National Park

Keibul Lamjao National Park is the only floating park in the world. A glimpse of the deer in this unique wetland ecosystem is a must for any wildlife enthusiast

Cultural Highlights

Manipur is a state in India with a rich cultural heritage, including temples, museums, and other cultural sites
